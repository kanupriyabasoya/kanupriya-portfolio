About Me

I'm Kanupriya Basoya — a visual artist and motion designer based in India. My work spans video editing and motion graphics for clients across the creative and tech industries.

I approach every project with one goal — make it work. Whether it's selling a product, explaining a concept, or telling a story, good video does the job without losing its soul. My process is rooted in research, experimentation, and a deep love for the craft. When I'm not editing, I'm watching films, obsessing over light, and finding new ways to make things look better.
